Potion Residues

Volatile & Rare Alchemical Byproducts

The dregs at the bottom of a cauldron contain highly concentrated, mutated magical properties from complex alchemical reactions.

Common Materials in this Category:

  • Ashwinder Egg Shell Fragments
  • Crystallized Polyjuice Sludge
  • Desiccated Lacewing Flies
  • Veritaserum Condensation Vials

Best Used For:

Experimental potioneers purchase residues to extract secondary compounds. Also used as extreme fertilizers for dangerous flora.
