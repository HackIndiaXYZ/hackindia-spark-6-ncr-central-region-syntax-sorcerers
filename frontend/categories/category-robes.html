Wizard Robes

Enchanted Textiles & Fabrics

Even the finest Madam Malkin's robes eventually fray or succumb to rogue spell damage. This category recycles magical textiles.

Common Materials in this Category:

  • Frayed Acromantula Silk Threads
  • Scorched Quidditch Leathers
  • Depleted Invisibility Cloak Patches
  • Standard Dragon-Hide Offcuts

Best Used For:

Magical tailors use scraps to patch garments or weave new fabrics. Excellent for Defense Against the Dark Arts target dummies.
