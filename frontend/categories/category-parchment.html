Parchment & Scrolls

Reclaimed Magical Stationery

The Ministry of Magic and Hogwarts generate thousands of scrolls daily. We collect, de-curse, and pulp them for reuse.

Common Materials in this Category:

  • Blanked Howler Paper (Slightly singed)
  • Misprinted Daily Prophet Rolls
  • Empty Spellbook Bindings
  • Discarded Ministry Memos

Best Used For:

Perfect for magical papier-mâché artists, Arithmancy students, or stationery shops pressing recycled parchment.
